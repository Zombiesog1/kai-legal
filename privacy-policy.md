# Privacy Policy for KAI

Effective Date: [05/03/2026]

KAI (“the App”) is a daily planner and performance tracker designed to help users organize tasks, track habits, complete focus sessions, and review daily progress.

## Information We Collect

KAI does not currently require account creation, login, or user registration.

The App may store information that you enter into the App, such as:
- Tasks
- Habits
- Focus session progress
- Daily review information
- Performance scores
- App preferences and settings

This information is stored locally on your device using local app storage.

## Local Data Storage

KAI stores user data locally on your device. This means your planner, habits, progress, and performance tracking data are saved on your device and are not currently uploaded to our servers.

If you delete the App or reset app data, your locally stored information may be deleted.

## Notifications

KAI may use local notifications to remind you about tasks, focus sessions, habits, or daily planning activities.

You can manage notification permissions through your device settings.

## Data Sharing

KAI does not sell your personal data.

KAI does not currently share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

## Analytics and Third-Party Services

KAI does not currently use account-based analytics, advertising tracking, or third-party data selling services.

If analytics, subscriptions, crash reporting, or other third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

## Children’s Privacy

KAI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can:
- Edit or delete tasks and habits inside the App
- Reset app data if the App provides that option
- Disable notifications in iOS Settings
- Delete the App to remove locally stored app data from your device

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
